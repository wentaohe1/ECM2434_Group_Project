Ecoffee is an application that allows you to track your coffee consumption, and watch the difference you make to the world and our climate. Our goal is to support the use of reusable solutions to modern day problems, starting with your morning coffee. We aim to help likeminded people connect and compete to save our climate and secure a green future for the coffee industry. So get yourself a reusable coffee cup and start saving our home, and maybe save some money along the way...
